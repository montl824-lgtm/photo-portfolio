ABOUT

JULIA ROCHA TURNER

Hi! I’m Julia — a 21-year-old photographer living in Bristol and studying at the University of West England. I was born in São Paulo, moved to the U.S. when I was five, and grew up in Richmond, Virginia. I’m majoring in fashion communications and photography, but I’m always experimenting with other creative hobbies like collaging, scrapbooking, sewing, and hunting for vintage clothes.

Some of my biggest inspirations are Michel Gondry and Clarice Lispector, and I love finding ways to blend those influences into my work. I’m always looking to meet new people in the field and take on new clients — if you’re interested, feel free to reach out via my email or Instagram linked on the contact page!
